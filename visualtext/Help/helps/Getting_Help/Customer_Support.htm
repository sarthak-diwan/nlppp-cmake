Assistance from TAI

TAI provides pre-built analyzers and knowledge bases to help jump start your analyzer development project. For example, we provide a generic analyzer called TAIParse that can be used as a starting point for many analyzers, in both restricted and broad domains.  TAIParse incorporates and utilizes a large knowledge base with dictionary information.  A knowledge base that embeds WordNet is available on request, as well.

We also offer training and professional services to accelerate the development of your analyzer.
